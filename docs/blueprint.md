# **App Name**: Carrusel Education

## Core Features:

- Homepage: Showcase institutional identity and quick access.
- About Us: Detail institutional mission, vision, and values.
- Educational Offer: Present the levels (Pre-school and Elementary) and pedagogical approach.
- Community: Highlight student, teacher, family, and administrative staff profiles.
- Gallery: Display photos and videos of institutional activities and events.
- News & Events: Maintain a blog-style section with announcements, celebrations, and important dates.
- Contact Information: Include address, phone number, email, map integration, and a quick contact form.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#0B3D91), derived from the logo's border, representing trust and stability.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F8FF), same hue family as the primary, for a clean and serene backdrop.
- Accent color: Dark purple (#4B0082), analogous to dark blue, but offering strong contrast for key interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, will give a precise, contemporary feel to headlines. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body font: 'Open Sans', a humanist sans-serif, will be used to keep text clean and readable. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Employ educational icons: microscopes, books, art palettes (taken from the logo).
- Ensure a responsive design: adaptable to mobile, tablet, and PC.
- Subtle transitions when switching between sections.
